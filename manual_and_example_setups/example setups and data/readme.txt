##### This directory contains the data and setups for the examples in Section 8 of the Zonation 5 manual.

### Data
The species data (species1.tif to species8.tif) are originally sourced from the Atlas of Living Australia (https://www.ala.org.au/) and BioNet (http://www.bionet.nsw.gov.au/). 
These and the environmental layers are a subset of data cleaned and collated by Kujala et al. (2015) (https://doi.org/10.1016/j.biocon.2015.08.017). 
We thank A. Whitehead, who compiled the original species data records and environmental variables.

Other included layers are arbitrary and were created for demonstration purposes.

### Setup folder - Section in manual

1_w - "8.1 Minimal setup"

2_wrg_wcrg - "8.2 Illustrating retention, condition and output groups"

3_dhW - "8.3 Illustrating hierarchic analysis, connectivity and weight groups"

4_sw - "8.4 Illustrating source-based impact assessment"

5_ew - "8.5 Illustrating cumulative impact assessment"
